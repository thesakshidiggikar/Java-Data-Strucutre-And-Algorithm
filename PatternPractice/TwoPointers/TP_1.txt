Problem:
Given a sorted array, find two numbers such that they add up to a specific target.

Pattern:
Two Pointers

Brute Force Idea:
Try all pairs and check sum.

Optimized Two Pointers Idea:
- One pointer at start, one at end
- If sum < target → move left
- If sum > target → move right

Time Complexity: O(n)
Space Complexity: O(1)

Edge Cases:
- No valid pair
